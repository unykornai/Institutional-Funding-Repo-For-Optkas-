## ACCESS CONTROLS

### Permission Levels

| Level | Who | Can See |
|:------|:----|:--------|
| **Full Access** | Lender deal team + counsel (post-NDA) | Folders 01â€“09 |
| **PII Access** | Compliance team only | Folder 06 (KYC/AML with individual PII) |
| **Lender-Specific** | Individual lender | Folder 10 (their correspondence only) |
| **Admin** | Borrower (jimmy@optkas.com) | All folders + upload/modify rights |

### Access Rules

1. **No lender sees another lender's correspondence** (Folder 10 is per-lender)
2. **PII documents require specific compliance request** (not shared on initial access)
3. **All access is view-only** â€” no downloads unless specifically authorized
4. **Watermarking enabled** on all PDF views (lender name + date)
5. **Access log maintained** â€” who viewed what, when

### Access Revocation

| Trigger | Action |
|:--------|:-------|
| NDA expiration | Auto-revoke all access |
| Lender declines | Revoke within 2 business days |
| Deal closes | Archive; retain per document retention policy |
| Borrower request | Admin can revoke at any time |
