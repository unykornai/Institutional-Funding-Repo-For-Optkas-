## ARTICLE II: MANAGEMENT

### 2.1 Manager
The Company shall be managed by **Jimmy** as the sole Manager (the "Manager").

### 2.2 Authority
The Manager shall have full authority to manage the business and affairs of the Company, including without limitation:
- (a) Executing facility agreements, security agreements, and control agreements;
- (b) Pledging Company assets as collateral for credit facilities;
- (c) Opening and managing bank and escrow accounts;
- (d) Engaging legal counsel, transfer agents, and other service providers;
- (e) Making all decisions regarding the day-to-day operations of the Company.

### 2.3 Delegation
The Manager may delegate authority to officers, agents, or representatives as the Manager deems appropriate.

## ARTICLE III: CAPITAL CONTRIBUTIONS

### 3.1 Initial Capital
The initial capital contribution shall be $1,000 by the Manager.

### 3.2 Additional Contributions
No member shall be required to make additional capital contributions without unanimous written consent.

## ARTICLE IV: DISTRIBUTIONS

### 4.1 Distributions
Distributions shall be made at the sole discretion of the Manager, subject to the Company's obligations under any facility agreements, security agreements, or other contractual commitments.

## ARTICLE VI: DISSOLUTION

### 6.1 Dissolution
